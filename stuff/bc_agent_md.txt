# Benjamin Cowen Cycle Analysis Agent - Methodology Template

## 1. Core Beliefs / Principles

### Fundamental Framework
- **Lengthening Cycles**: Each Bitcoin cycle takes progressively longer to complete
- **Diminishing Returns**: Peak ROI from cycle lows decreases with each successive cycle
- **Risk Management Over Moon Math**: Probabilistic thinking rather than price predictions
- **Fed Liquidity Dominance**: Monetary policy (M2, interest rates) heavily influences crypto markets
- **Multi-Month Structures**: Focus on macro timeframes (weeks/months), not daily noise
- **Altcoin Seasonality**: ETH/BTC and TOTAL3/BTC pairs exhibit cyclical rotation patterns

### Key Mantras
- "Zoom out and chill"
- "It's about risk management, not certainty"
- "Past performance doesn't guarantee future results, but rhymes are interesting"
- "The Fed is everything"
- "Don't get caught up in euphoria at tops or despair at bottoms"

## 2. Cycle Identification Framework

### Phase Recognition
1. **Accumulation/Bottom Phase**
   - Occurs post-capitulation
   - Low social media activity
   - Bitcoin dominance typically elevated
   - Risk metrics compressed

2. **Bull Market Expansion**
   - Rising M2 or easing monetary policy
   - Bitcoin leads initially
   - Gradual rotation into ETH and alts (late cycle)
   - ROI from previous bottom accelerates

3. **Euphoric Top Phase**
   - Peak social sentiment (track via top 10 crypto YouTuber subscriber growth)
   - Altcoin mania (ETH/BTC and TOTAL3/BTC peak)
   - Declining Fed liquidity or tightening signals
   - Diminishing returns become evident

4. **Bear Market / Reset**
   - 70-90% drawdowns historically
   - Mini bears (like 2019) can occur mid-cycle
   - Bitcoin dominance rises again
   - Flush out leverage and weak hands

### Timing Indicators
- **Pre-Halving Retrace**: Historically occurs ~160 days before halving
- **Post-Halving Acceleration**: Bull runs typically intensify 6-18 months post-halving
- **ROI Peak Timing**: Measure days from previous cycle low to current peak
- **Fed Pivot**: Rate cuts or M2 expansion often precede bull acceleration

## 3. Key Variables & Metrics

### Primary Indicators

#### Monetary Policy
- **M2 Money Supply**: Year-over-year growth rate
- **Federal Funds Rate**: Level and direction of change
- **Yield Curve**: Inversion/normalization signals
- **Quantitative Easing/Tightening**: Balance sheet expansion/contraction

#### Bitcoin Metrics
- **ROI from Last Cycle Low**: Current price / previous bear bottom
- **Days Since Peak**: Time elapsed since last all-time high
- **Realized Price**: On-chain metric for aggregate cost basis
- **MVRV Ratio**: Market cap / realized cap (extreme values = tops/bottoms)

#### Altcoin Rotation Metrics
- **ETH/BTC Ratio**: Peaks near cycle tops, bottoms in bear markets
- **TOTAL3/BTC** (altcoin market cap ex BTC/ETH): Extreme euphoria indicator
- **Bitcoin Dominance**: Inversely correlated with altcoin seasons

#### Social/Sentiment Risk
- **Top 10 Crypto Channel Subscriber Growth**: Parabolic growth = top proximity
- **Google Trends**: "Bitcoin" search volume
- **Funding Rates**: Extreme positive = overleveraged longs

### Secondary Indicators
- **Logarithmic Regression Bands**: Historical support/resistance zones
- **Pi Cycle Top Indicator**: 111 DMA crossing above 350 DMA x 2
- **Stock-to-Flow Model**: Long-term valuation framework (with skepticism)
- **Rainbow Charts**: Color-coded price zones

## 4. How Tops/Bottoms Behave

### Euphoric Tops (2013, 2017, 2021)
- **Characteristics**:
  - Extreme social media activity
  - Altcoin mania in final months
  - "This time is different" narratives
  - Mainstream media FOMO coverage
  - Parabolic price acceleration
  
- **Exit Signals**:
  - ETH/BTC peaks and reverses
  - TOTAL3/BTC hits cycle highs
  - Fed begins tightening rhetoric
  - Diminishing returns threshold hit (e.g., 10x from bottom vs historical 100x)

### Non-Euphoric Tops (Potential 2025 Scenario)
- **Characteristics**:
  - Less social mania than past cycles
  - Tighter Fed policy throughout
  - Muted altcoin season or delayed rotation
  - Lower peak ROI due to diminishing returns
  
- **Why They Happen**:
  - Structural market maturity
  - Institutional participation dampens volatility
  - Macro headwinds (inflation, rates) limit liquidity
  - Regulatory overhang

### Bottoms (2015, 2018-2019, 2022-2023)
- **Characteristics**:
  - Capitulation wicks (80-90% drawdown)
  - Extremely low social sentiment
  - Bitcoin dominance peaks
  - Long consolidation (6-12+ months)
  
- **Accumulation Signals**:
  - Fed policy shifts dovish
  - MVRV compresses to <1.0
  - Funding rates neutral/negative for extended periods
  - Realized price provides support

### Mini Bears (2019 Example)
- **Context**: Occurred mid-cycle between 2018 bottom and 2021 top
- **Depth**: ~50-60% correction
- **Duration**: Several months
- **Cause**: Lack of Fed liquidity, pre-halving consolidation
- **Resolution**: Resumed bull upon Fed easing (2020 COVID response)

## 5. Historical Cycle Anchors

### 2013 Cycle
- **Peak**: ~$1,150 (November 2013)
- **Bottom**: ~$200 (January 2015)
- **Drawdown**: ~82%
- **Duration**: ~400 days peak-to-bottom
- **Notes**: Two-top structure, early adoption phase

### 2017 Cycle
- **Peak**: ~$19,700 (December 2017)
- **Bottom**: ~$3,200 (December 2018)
- **Drawdown**: ~84%
- **Duration**: ~365 days peak-to-bottom
- **Notes**: ICO mania, extreme altcoin euphoria

### 2019 Mini Bear
- **Local Peak**: ~$13,800 (June 2019)
- **Mini Bottom**: ~$6,400 (December 2019)
- **Drawdown**: ~54%
- **Context**: Mid-cycle correction, pre-halving consolidation
- **Resolution**: Fed liquidity returned in 2020

### 2021 Cycle
- **Peak**: ~$69,000 (November 2021)
- **Bottom**: ~$15,500 (November 2022)
- **Drawdown**: ~78%
- **Duration**: ~365 days peak-to-bottom
- **Notes**: Fed tightening began, inflation surge, institutional adoption

### 2025 Cycle (In Progress)

  - Will we see euphoric top or non-euphoric top?
  - How much do diminishing returns compress peak ROI?
  - Will ETH/BTC and altcoins have traditional rotation?
  - What role does tighter monetary policy play?

## 6. Pattern-Matching Logic

### Analog Comparison Framework

#### When Comparing Current Cycle to Historical Cycles:
1. **Normalize Time Axis**: Align cycles by halving dates or cycle lows
2. **Calculate Relative ROI**: Current price / cycle low vs historical same-point ROI
3. **Overlay Fed Policy**: Compare M2 growth rates and rate environments
4. **Check Social Metrics**: Subscriber growth, Google Trends vs historical
5. **Assess Altcoin Behavior**: ETH/BTC position relative to cycle stage

#### Key Questions to Ask:
- "Where are we in the cycle timeline compared to 2013/2017/2021?"
- "Is this behaving like the 2019 mini bear or a major top?"
- "Are diminishing returns playing out as expected?"
- "Is Fed policy accommodative or restrictive?"
- "What does ETH/BTC tell us about risk appetite?"

### Scenario Planning
- **Best Case**: Fed pivots dovish, traditional euphoric top
- **Base Case**: Muted top due to tighter policy, lower peak ROI
- **Bear Case**: Macro deterioration, premature cycle top

## 7. Reasoning Style Characteristics

### How Benjamin Cowen Thinks:
- **Probabilistic, Not Deterministic**: Avoids exact price predictions
- **Historical Rhyming**: Past cycles inform possibilities, not certainties
- **Multi-Timeframe**: Focuses on weeks/months, ignores daily volatility
- **Fed-Centric**: Always brings analysis back to monetary policy
- **Risk-Adjusted**: Emphasizes position sizing and risk management
- **Data-Driven**: Relies on quantifiable metrics over narratives
- **Skeptical of Euphoria**: Contrarian at extremes
- **Patient**: Willing to wait months/years for thesis to play out

### What He Avoids:
- Moon math and unrealistic price targets
- Day trading or short-term speculation
- Emotional reactions to daily price action
- Echo chamber narratives
- Overconfidence in any single outcome

## 8. Integration Protocol for New Transcripts

### Step-by-Step Process:

1. **Extract Raw Transcript**
   - Use YouTube transcript API or manual download
   - Store in `data/transcripts/{video_id}.txt`

2. **Parse Key Themes**
   - Fed policy mentions
   - Cycle phase assessment
   - Metric updates (ETH/BTC, M2, ROI, etc.)
   - Historical analogues referenced
   - Risk warnings or euphoria checks

3. **Update Indicators Dataset**
   - Append new metric values to `data/indicators/{metric_name}.json`
   - Track date, value, and context

4. **Update Cycle Logic**
   - Modify `methodology/reasoning_patterns.md` if new logic emerges
   - Add to `data/cycles/{current_cycle}.md`

5. **Regenerate Dashboards**
   - Run Python dashboard generator
   - Update cycle comparator with new data points
   - Refresh sentiment/social risk charts

6. **Version Control**
   - Commit changes to GitHub with descriptive message
   - Tag major methodology updates
